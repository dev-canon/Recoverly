What is Recoverly?

Recoverly is an automated tool for Shopify stores that helps you recover lost sales from abandoned carts. It sends personalized emails, SMS, and WhatsApp messages to customers who leave without buying—bringing them back to complete their purchase.

  • • Boost your revenue by recovering 10–20% of lost sales
